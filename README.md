# Santi AI

**Your screen. Your voice. Your AI.**

A desktop AI companion that sees what you're working on and responds by voice.
No browser tabs, no prompts to type, no context to copy-paste.

### Requirements

- macOS 13+ or Windows 10+
- Microphone access
- Internet connection

### What it does

- **Screen Vision** — Share your display and Santi understands the full context
- **Voice Interface** — Hold a key to talk, Santi responds by voice in real time
- **Always Available** — Lives in your menu bar, one keypress away
