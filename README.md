## What is Santi AI?

Santi AI is a desktop application that combines real-time voice conversation with screen understanding. It sees what you see, listens when you talk, and responds by voice instantly.

Built for professionals who spend their day navigating complex workflows: spreadsheets, trading platforms, CRMs, documents, code. Instead of switching to a browser tab and typing a prompt, you talk.

<br>

## How it works

| Step | What happens |
|---|---|
| **1. Open Santi AI** | Launch from your menu bar or dock. Starts instantly, stays ready in the background. |
| **2. Share your screen** | One click. Santi sees everything you see and understands the context. |
| **3. Talk** | Hold to talk, release to listen. Santi responds by voice with full awareness of your screen. |

<br>

## Capabilities

**Screen Vision** &mdash; Share your display and Santi understands the full context. Spreadsheets, trading platforms, documents, presentations, code. Ask about what's on screen and get precise answers.

**Voice Interface** &mdash; Natural conversation with sub-second responses. Built on Gemini Live for real-time multimodal understanding. No typing required.

**Always Available** &mdash; Lives in your menu bar. One keypress away, in any application. No app switching, no workflow interruption.

## Requirements

- Microphone access (for voice conversations)
- Internet connection (for AI processing)
- Screen sharing permission (optional, for screen vision)

<br>

## Tech

Built with [Tauri](https://tauri.app) for native performance. 3 MB instead of 150+ MB. Uses the system's native webview, not a bundled browser engine.

Voice and vision powered by [Gemini Live API](https://ai.google.dev) for real-time multimodal understanding.

<br>

## Privacy

- Voice is processed in real-time, not stored
- Screen frames are sent only while sharing is active
- No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking
- No account required
